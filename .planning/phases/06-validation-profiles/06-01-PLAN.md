       - DOC Survey: ranges doc={0, 3}, depth={0, 500}, easting={100000, 900000}, northing={0, 10000000}. All checks enabled.
       - TOP Survey: ranges top={-200, 200}, depth={0, 500}, easting={100000, 900000}, northing={0, 10000000}. All checks enabled.
